Huevos con Salchicha

Description

This is going to be a recipe for a staple mexican breakfast.

It is a simple recipe but this always hits the spot and satisfies.

Ingredients

  • 2 Sausages
  • 3 eggs
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Cooking Steps

  • 1. Start off by putting some oil in a pan to heat up. Usually need about 2 tablespoons give or take.
  • 2. While oil is heating up, cut the sausages into small circles.
  • 3. Once the oil is heated up, put the sausages in the pan and cook them for about 2 minutes.
  • 4. Once the sausages are cooked, add in the 3 eggs and salt & pepper to taste.
  • 5. The eggs are scrambled. Once cooked take of the pan, serve and enjoy!
  • Side Note: You can eat them with some tortillas and some tapatio. Some ppl also sometime adds ketchup but not me tho. To each their own.
